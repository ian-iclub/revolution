About AMUA

Amua is a web based data analytics platform for quickly analysing the financial data and behaviour contained in M-Pesa and bank statements.

  • Time-consuming manual checks.
  • High risk of errors.
  • Prone to biased credit decisions.


    • With Amua lending is made more fast & efficient.

    How It Works

    Grabs M-PESA statement without risk of manipulation.

    Allows credit decision making (incl. limit, pricing).

    Analyses financial data and behaviour of loan applicants.

    Initiates and tracks loan disbursement.

    Business Intelligence Dashboard Risk Analytics Deeper Customer Insights Predictive Analytics Automated Report Generator Fraud Prevention & Detection

    Business Intelligence Dashboard

    Displays extracted data in a meaningful way with an overview of all historical transactions.

    Risk Analytics

    Enables you to know in advance, possible defaulters based on historical transaction data.

    Deeper Customer Insights

    Captures financial and non-financial data which are not included in a CRB report.

    Predictive Analytics

    Groups patterns into specific clusters, enabling you to predict future customer behaviour and product needs.

    Automated Report Generator

    Generates a downloadable report based on processed data and expert knowledge from loan officer.

    Fraud Prevention & Detection

    Able to profile and detect fraudulent behaviour based on historical transaction data on M-PESA or Bank Statement.
